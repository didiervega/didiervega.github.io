About Me

Welcome to my professional home page!. I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow (FAPESP) at the Department of Computer Sciences, University of São Paulo. I am also a collaborative research at the National Institute for Space Research - INPE, working at the Laboratory of Computation and Applied Mathematics. My research activities are in complex networks, social networks analisys, data mining and information spreading models applied to big data. Currently, my fields of interests encompass machine learning based on graphs, modeling climate systems and disasters risk reduction and management. If you need further information or are interested in collaboration with us, please don't hesitate to contact me.
